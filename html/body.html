Welcome to CAPE!
Cape is a comprehensive website designed to provide programming learners, especially students at school, with timely assistance from teachers and other learners with coding and debugging. Cape has three major fuction sections including a co-editor, personal notebook and a forum. Detailed introdution are below:
Introdution of Co-editor:Live share your IDE for real-time collaborative development
Co-editor is an editor that enables three programmers to code together in a certain section, which is a perfect blend of functionality and simplicity, designed by a team of remote developers that rely on collaborative development.
Whether you are in a team that uses pair programming as part of your regular software development flow or you just a student/learner want others to help you find your errors and bugs, Co-editor is the best tool for pair programming, mob programming, code review, and more!
